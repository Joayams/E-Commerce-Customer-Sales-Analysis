E-Commerce Customer & Sales Analysis

Project Overview

This Business Intelligence project analyzes customer purchasing behavior, sales performance, and product trends using SQL and Power BI. The objective is to transform raw business data into actionable insights that support strategic business decisions.

Business Questions

During this project, the following business questions were answered:

•	Who are the company's top customers? 

•	Which product categories generate the highest revenue? 

•	Which countries contribute the most revenue?

•	Which membership tier generates the highest revenue?

•	Which customers are at risk of churn? 

•	How has revenue changed over time?

Key Business Insights

💰 Revenue by Product Category

Insight

Electronics generated the highest revenue, followed by Clothing & Apparel and Home & Kitchen.

Recommendation

Continue investing in high-performing categories while investigating lower-performing categories for growth opportunities.

________________________________________

🌍 Revenue by Country

Insight

Revenue is concentrated in a few countries, indicating stronger market performance in those regions.

Recommendation

Increase marketing investment in high-performing countries while identifying opportunities to improve sales in lower-performing markets.

________________________________________

👑 Revenue by Membership Tier

Insight

The Free membership tier generated the highest overall revenue.

Recommendation

Analyze why Free members spend the most and develop strategies to convert these customers into paid membership tiers.

________________________________________

👤 Top Customers

Insight

A small group of customers contributes a significant portion of total revenue.

Recommendation

Reward these customers with loyalty programs, exclusive promotions, or VIP benefits to improve retention.

________________________________________

📈 Monthly Revenue Trend

Insight

Revenue fluctuated throughout the year, with noticeable peaks and declines.

Recommendation

Investigate the factors influencing seasonal performance and use promotional campaigns during slower months.

________________________________________

⏳ Customer Retention

Insight

Several customers have not purchased in over 180 days, indicating a high risk of churn.

Recommendation

Launch targeted email campaigns and special promotions to re-engage inactive customers.
